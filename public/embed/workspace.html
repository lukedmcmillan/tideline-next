OSPAR Consultation Response: Article 9
Overview
OSPAR's revised Article 9 framework proposes extending recovery zone boundaries by 40% across the North-East Atlantic. The scientific basis draws directly on 2025 ICES advisory findings on stock recovery timelines. Consultation closes 8 April 2026.

OSPAR revised North-East Atlantic Fisheries Recovery Zones: Article 9
OSPAR Commission · 15 Mar 2026 · Tier 1
Revised framework proposes 40% boundary extension for fisheries recovery zones. Cites ICES 2025 advisory opinion on North-East Atlantic stock recovery timelines as scientific basis.
Scientific Basis
Tideline Research: primary sources only
Query: What scientific basis does OSPAR cite for the 40% boundary extension?
OSPAR cites the 2025 ICES advisory opinion on North-East Atlantic stock recovery timelines, which found existing recovery zones insufficient to achieve sustainable yield by 2030 targets. The boundary extension is specifically linked to the ICES habitat classification update of January 2026. Source: OSPAR Commission, Mar 2026 · Tier 1.
Our response should address the ICES scientific basis directly. The 2030 target alignment creates a strong obligation argument for supporting the extension.
