# Tideline Messaging House
## Locked March 2026 — Reference before writing any copy

---

## NON-NEGOTIABLE RULES
- No em dashes. Ever. Use a full stop, a colon, or rewrite the sentence.
- No "AI" language. No "AI-powered", "intelligent", "smart". Say what it does.
- No "7am" as a selling point. It is a delivery detail, not a feature.
- No environmental cause language. Tideline is for professionals, not campaigners.
- No feature lists in headlines. Name the person or the pain, not the product.
- No exclamation marks. Ever.
- No "we" in product copy. Use "Tideline" or "your" only.
- No "newsletter". Tideline is not a newsletter.
- No "cutting-edge", "revolutionary", "game-changer", "passionate about".
